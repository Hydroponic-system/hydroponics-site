Product Description

High-Performance NFT Solution

NFT (Nutrient Film Technique) is the most popular hydroponic method for commercial leafy green production. Our professional-grade PVC channels are designed with a unique "Nutrient Film" profile to ensure optimal root aeration and nutrient uptake.

  • UV-Stabilized Food Grade PVC
