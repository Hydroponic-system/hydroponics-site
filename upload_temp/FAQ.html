Frequently Asked
Questions

Common questions about our products, shipping, and professional project support.

Q1: Do you support OEM/ODM customization?

Yes, absolutely. We provide professional OEM and ODM services. We can customize system dimensions, materials, and branding according to your project requirements.

Q2: What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ depends on the specific product. For standard hydroponic systems, the MOQ is usually 10 units, while for greenhouse structures, we handle projects starting from 500 square meters.

Q3: How long is the delivery time?

For in-stock items, delivery takes 7-10 working days. For bulk orders or customized projects, the lead time is typically 20-35 working days depending on the project scale.

Q4: Do you provide installation guidance?

Yes. We provide detailed installation manuals, 3D technical drawings, and instructional videos. For large-scale projects, we can also provide online engineering support or field technicians if required.

Q5: What are the payment terms?

Standard terms are 30% T/T deposit and 70% balance before shipment. We also accept L/C and Alibaba Trade Assurance for secure global transactions.
