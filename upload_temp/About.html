Agri Hydroponics was founded with a single mission: to provide professional, sustainable, and high-yield agricultural solutions to growers worldwide. Based in China's manufacturing heartland, we have grown from a local supplier to a global exporter serving over 100 countries.

We specialize in the R&D, design, and manufacturing of greenhouse structures and advanced hydroponic systems, including Vertical Towers, NFT Channels, and Dutch Bucket systems. Our factory integrates strict QC standards with innovative engineering to ensure every project succeeds.
